The Why?

We believe in making our life much easier and efficient with the help of technology and improving our market status while having a great work-life balance.

The How?

By understanding the tech market in Egypt and the Middle East we laverage the insights in our markets and the work environments challenges.

The What?

Revealing the survey insights will empower both geeks and startups with the annual technology trends and the market status in our region.
We are collecting data from over 50,000 developers, asking them questions on topics ranging from programing languages, frameworks to work environment and more.

The survey has started and it will be on for a week. The survey results will be published on github once we finish the insights report.
